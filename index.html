Slow & Reverb Music

Heart Touching

Slowed + Reverb is remixing technique popular among amateur music producers that involves slowing the tempo of a song down and adding a reverb effect to it. The technique is a simpler version of the chopped and screwed technique developed in the 1990s by DJ Screw and has been called a "gentrified" version of it.
